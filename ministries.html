Celebrate Recovery

Celebrate Recovery is a Christ-Centered recovery program.

Based on the 12-Steps developed by Bill Wilson for Alcoholics Anonymous,

Celebrate Recovery applies the time-tested principles underlying 12-step programs,

and plants these principles in the rich soil of scripture,

resulting in a harvest of new life for those seeking freedom!
